--- layout: post title: Density plots in R date: '2010-12-16T09:55:00.000-08:00' author: Mark Evans tags: - density plot - R modified_time: '2010-12-16T09:58:18.074-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8042776731714569314.post-4712911970517763453 blogger_orig_url: http://codexpython.blogspot.com/2010/12/density-plots-in-r.html --- To plot the distribution of scores in R using the density function, use

plot(density(dat$scores))
To overlay scores for another variable onto the first plot, use

lines(density(dat$other_scores), col="red")
Simple enough!
